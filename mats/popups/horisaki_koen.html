Horisaki Koen Soccer Ground Saitama
  • Surface: Large new generation artificial all weather pitch.
  • Facilities: Changing roomsbut NO showers, toilets & lockers. Vending machines outside of main building for drinks. Tel:
  • Parking: Free car park next to ground
  • Getting There:
    BY TRAIN: The ground is 10 mins walk from Owada Station on the Tobu Noda line (6 mins from Omiya).
    After exiting the station-  Turn right at a shop called "masuya". keep walking for 10 minutes and should see the pitch on your left.
  • Rules: When teams & supporters leave after the match, they will make sure to clean up around the ground and changing rooms, removing all rubbish. Please follow all regulations at grounds (especially smoking!).
