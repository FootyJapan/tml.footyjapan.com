Enjoy Guinness Festival 2004

Drink 6 Draught Guinness pints and win a chance to play 4 on 4 soccer with former professional soccer players!

50 teams will be randomly selected to participate in the sans GK "4 on 4 soccer tournament" and get a chance to play with former soccer players such as Masami Ihara (Urawa Reds), Takuya Takagi (San Frecce Hiroshima), Masahiro Fukuda (Urawa Reds). 6 Draught Guinness proof of purchase stickers necessary to enter.

Drink 2 Draught Guinness Pints and win an original T-shirt!

1,000 people will be randomly selected to receive an original Guinness T-shirt.
Enjoy Guinness Festival 2004 campaign details

  • Campaign deadline - May 18, 2004 (Tue.). Valid if postmarked by said date.
  • How to apply - You will receive a sticker for each Guinness you order. Affix the stickers onto the official entry postcard and fill in the appropriate details to apply.
  • Choice selection
  • Choice A. 4 on 4 Soccer. 6 proof of purchase stickers required for one entry. 50 teams will be randomly selected to participate in the tournament.
  • Choice B. Original Guinness T-Shirt. 2 proof of purchase stickers for one entry. 1,000 people will receive an original Guinness T-shirt.
  • Mailing address:
    "Enjoy Guinness Festival 2004" campaign office
    Tokyo Chuo Post Office
    P.O. Box 480. 100-8663
  • Notification of winners:
    Choice A winners will be notified directly by the campaign office. Choice B winners will receive their original Guinness T-shirt by mail.

"Draught Guinness 4 on 4 soccer tournament" details

  • Event dates: June 5, 2004 (Sat.), June 6, 2004 (Sun.) 10:00 to 18:00 (scheduled). Event will take place even under rainy conditions.
  • Venue: JR Tokyo Station Yaesu North entrance court
  • Game format: 15 minute games, tournament style. (There will also be an award ceremony.) Minimum of 4 players per team, maximum of 6 players. All players must be over 20 years of age.
  • Participating players: Masami Ihara (Formerly of Urawa Reds), Takuya Takagi (Formerly of San Frecce Hiroshima), Masahiro Fukuda (Formerly of Urawa Reds), Takeshi Mizuuchi (Formerly of Urawa Reds), Takehito Suzuki (Formerly of Yokohama F Marinos), Satoru Kobayashi (Formerly of Omiya Ardija), Nobuyuki Hosaka (Formerly of Urawa Reds), Junichi Watanabe (Formerly of Verdy Tokyo), Jo Nakajima (Formerly of Kashima Antlers), Yukinori Muramatsu (Formerly of Urawa Reds), Takashi Sakurai (Formerly of Yokohama Flugels).
  • *Please note participating players may change without prior notice.
