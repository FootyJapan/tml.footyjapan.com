Nagahama Koen (Yokohama FC training ground)
  • Surface: Large natural grass football pitch, beautiful condition but often only half pitch used, can be used 7:00am - 5:00pm .
  • Facilities: Small changing rooms, toilets, coin showers. Vending machines outside of ground for drinks.
  • Parking: Yes, nearby parking lot.
  • Location: Take Keikyu Zushi line to Keikyu Tomioka station (10 minute walk) or take Yokohama seaside line to Namiki Chuo station MAP
    For car Navi: 045-782-8004
    Ground Website
  • Rules: When teams & supporters leave after the match, they will make sure to clean up around the ground and changing rooms, removing all rubbish. Please follow all regulations at grounds (especially smoking!).
