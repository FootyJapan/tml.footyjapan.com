BECOME A QUALIFIED ENGLISH FA COACH THIS GOLDEN WEEK!!

Following the success of the Level 1 and Level 2 FA coaching courses tutored by The FA's venerable Steven Lister (UEFA 'A' Pro License) in Japan in 2006 and 2007, the Sheffield & Hallamshire CFA in Association with Footy Japan will once again be hosting the hugely popular FA Level 1 Club Coach course at the end of April ~ beginning May 2008, this time tutored by one of Steven Lister's counterparts, Michael Matthews, also a holder of his UEFA 'A' Pro License and a former professional player at Wolverhampton Wanderers FC.

17,000 people have registered to take this course in the last 12-months in the UK and it's popularity means that enrolling on this course in the UK can take time. Thankfully the FA has granted the Sheffield & Hallamshire CFA and Footy Japan KK. official permission to bring this course to your doorstep - it is indeed a rare occurrence for an 'accredited' English FA coaching course to be conducted outside the UK - presenting a golden opportunity for future coaches to get their first globally recognized coaching license this Golden week!! The FA Level 1 Club Coach qualification is divided into three sections:

  • Theory/Emergengy Aid) = classroom time
  • Practical = time spent on football pitch
  • Final Assessments = practical examinations (on the pitch).

Available spaces on the course are limited to maintain a low coach : tutor ratio, enrollment will take place on a first-come-first-served basis.
