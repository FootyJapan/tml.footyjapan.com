Ina Saitama ground
  • Surface: Hard dirt. JET's home ground. 9:00am - 5:00pm.
  • Facilities: Changing room, toilets, showers. Vending machines outside of ground for drinks.
  • Parking: Yes.
  • Location: Take JR to Omiya Saitama (35 mins from Shinjuku or Ueno).Change to the new Shuttle (monorail) to Hanuki (25 mins). From Hanuki Station there is a free shuttle bus to the Kenmin katsudo Sogo Centre(4 mins).

  • Rules:. When teams & supporters leave after the match, they will make sure to clean up around the ground and changing rooms, removing all rubbish. Please follow all regulations at grounds (especially smoking!)
