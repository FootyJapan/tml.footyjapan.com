American School in Japan (ASIJ) Soccer Ground
  • Surface: Large new generation artificial all weather/purpose pitch, Has markings for Soccer, Rugby & American football.
  • Facilities: Changing rooms with hot showers, toilets & lockers. Vending machines inside of main building for drinks. ASIJ Website Tel: 0422-34-5300
  • Parking: Carpark 100 yards from main entrance.
  • Getting There:
    BY CAR: Getting to the Chofu Campus from downtown Tokyo. Take Shuto No.4 (which merges into the Chuo Expressway) to the Chofu Interchange (Exit No.3). Exit toward Tachikawa and Fuchu onto Route 20. Turn right off Route 20 at the Ajinomoto Stadium. From there, follow the map above.

    BY TRAIN: Take the Chuo Line (the orange train) toward Tachikawa and Takao. Get off at Musashisakai Station, one stop after Mitaka. (If on a limited express [tokubetsu kaisoku], the Tozai Line or Sobu Line, get off at Mitaka and transfer to the Chuo Line. Go one stop and get off at Musashisakai.) At Musashisakai, exit the JR station and re-enter the Seibu Line station via the south exit, take the Seibu Tamagawa Line to Tama Station, the second stop. The Seibu Tamagawa Line runs every 12 minutes for most of the day. At Tama Station, follow the map above. The school is a 5 minute walk from the station.

    BY TAXI: If you wish, taxis are available at the south exit of Musashisakai Station or the east exit of Tama Station.

  • Rules: When teams & supporters leave after the match, they will make sure to clean up around the ground and changing rooms, removing all rubbish. Please follow all regulations at grounds (especially smoking!).
