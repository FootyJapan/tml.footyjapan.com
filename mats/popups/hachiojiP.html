Hachioji Park, Saitama Soccer Ground
  • Surface: Large artificial floodlit football pitch, can be used 9:00am - 9:00pm. Nice, soft surface (moulded stud ok)
  • Facilities: Changing rooms with hot showers, toilets & ¥100 lockers. Vending machines inside of main building for drinks.
  • Parking: Carpark inside complex.
  • Location: 10 minutes taxi from Yonohommachi Station on the JR Saikyo MAP for car Navi : 048-856-1313
  • Rules: When teams & supporters leave after the match, they will make sure to clean up around the ground and changing rooms, removing all rubbish. Please follow all regulations at grounds (especially smoking!).
