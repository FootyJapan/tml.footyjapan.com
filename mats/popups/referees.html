REFEREES WANTED

Can you control 22+ whinging adult players?
Footy Japan is always looking for new referees to officiate matches
in the Tokyo Metropolis League, FJ Cup and one day,
small sided tournaments

QUALIFICATIONS: Preferably we'd like qualified referees holding a licence, but
we will consider new referees who have experience refereeing
international matches and a good knowledge of the game
All referees MUST be able to speak English, any other languages a bonus!

LOCATION: Most of our matches take place in 3 locations around Tokyo,
Yokohama, Saitama and Chiba. For a full list of venues please visit: Fixtures page

MATCHES: Where possible, we try to give referees 2 matches back to back
as travelling distances can be quite far

UNIFORM: Referees are required to have their own referee uniform,
whistle & flags. (We can lend equipment for their first matches if necessary)

REMUNERATION: We pay our referees ¥6,000 minus tax, per match plus
reasonable travel expenses (trains or parking) at the end of each month

TRIAL: All new referees will be given an UNPAID trial match (Where possible a friendly
match not league or cup) then if they are suitable they will be schedules regular league matches.

CONTACT: If you are interested in trying out to become a Footy Japan
referee, then please contact us at: referees@footyjapan.com
supplying us with: Name, Age, Nationality, Occupation,
Ref qualifications and/or experience, Telephone number, em@il,
address, nearest station, availability (times & days).
