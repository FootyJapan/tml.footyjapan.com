Fujitsu Big Circus Sports Center
  • Surface: Large artificial floodlit pitch, can be used 9:00am - 9:00pm. Hard surface (moulded stud ok but turf shoes better!)
  • Facilities: Changing rooms with hot showers, toilets & lockers. Vending machines inside of main building for drinks. Tel: 046-242-8611
  • Parking: Carpark inside complex.
  • Location: 10 minutes by taxi from Hon Atsugi Station on the Odakyu line MAP
  • Rules: When teams & supporters leave after the match, they will make sure to clean up around the ground and changing rooms, removing all rubbish. Please follow all regulations at grounds (especially smoking!).
