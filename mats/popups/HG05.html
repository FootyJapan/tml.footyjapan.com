THE 23rd HIGHLAND GAMES

General Rules

  1. Group matches will be 7 mins each half with a 1 minute turn around
  2. Semi-Finals and Finals will be 10 mins each half.
  3. Teams will arrive at the ground at 9:00am in preparation for a 9:30 kick off
  4. Entry fee of ¥10,000 per team plus ¥1,500 per person to enter the Highland Games. This is all for Charity, please go to the Highland Games website for more details.
  5. All teams will be expected to provide a referee for a couple of games, this will be on the schedule on the day.
  6. Usual FIFA rules for the matches, except; kick-ins instead of throw-ins, no off-side, keeper throws must bounce in own half (no drop kicks)
  7. Masters tournament is for over 35's, Ladies & Social teams. Teams will be allowed to field a couple of under 35 'social' players, but no 'ringers'. This is aimed at helping some of the sponsors teams, so please use sportsmanship and don't abuse this rule.
  8. All players must have their own insurance as Footy Japan will not be responsible for any injuries incurred during the games.
  9. This is a family, Charity event, so players are asked to curb their use of 'colorful' language as ladies and children will be present. Also on field we would like to see fair play and understanding if officials make mistakes.
