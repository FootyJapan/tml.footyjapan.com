Yoyogi NHK ground
  • Surface: Large dirt pitch, can be used 9:00am - 6:00pm. Gets very dusty in summer.
  • Facilities: Changing rooms with hot showers, toilets & ¥100 lockers at sports arena next door. Vending machines outside of ground for drinks.
  • Parking: Meter parking on main road near to ground.
  • Location: 7 minutes walk from Harajuku Station on the Yamanote line. MAP or for Yahoo driving directions, CLICK HERE
  • Rules: When teams & supporters leave after the match, they will make sure to clean up around the ground and changing rooms, removing all rubbish. Please follow all regulations at grounds (especially smoking!).
