Misato Asahi Shimbun Soccer Grounds
  • Surface: 3 grass pitches can be used 9:00am - 5:00pm. (Often prone to flooding & cancellation.) Slippery clay surface under the grass.
  • Facilities: Hot showers & toilets in main sports building.
  • Parking: Cars can be parked next to ground. tel: 0489-57-2074
  • Location: 15 minutes walk from Misato Station on the Musashino line MAP
  • Rules: Nets & flags must be taken & set up for first match of day & returned after last (teams please assist in this). When teams & supporters leave after the match, they will make sure to clean up around the ground and changing rooms, removing all rubbish. Please follow all regulations at grounds (especially smoking!).
