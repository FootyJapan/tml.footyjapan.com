Daito Bunka University Soccer Ground
  • Surface: Large artificial turf rugby ground.
  • Facilities: Changing rooms with hot showers & lockers & ONLY available Saturdays. Vending machines outside of ground for drinks.
  • Parking: limited parking spaces on campus.
  • Location: 7 minutes by bus from the east exit of Takasaka Station on the Tobu Tojo Line (FREE school shuttle bus available Saturdays!).
