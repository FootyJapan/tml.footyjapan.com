Shin-Yokohama park, Undo-hiroba Soccer Ground
  • Surface: Large clay pitch, can be used 9:00am - 5:00pm.
    Ground can be closed in event of heavy rain.
  • Facilities: Changing rooms with showers, toilets & lockers. They shut the building with the showers, vending machines etc at 5pm on the dot.
    Telephone: 045-477-5146
    Ground website: http://www.city.yokohama.jp/me/kankyou/park/shinyoko/index.html
  • Parking: Carpark near ground.
  • Location: 5 minutes walk from JR Kozukue station on the JR Yokohama line MAP
  • Rules: When teams & supporters leave after the match, they will make sure to clean up around the ground and changing rooms, removing all rubbish. Please follow all regulations at grounds (especially smoking!).
