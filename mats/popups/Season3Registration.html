How to register for TML Season 3

The following is a quick guide to registering your team and your members with the TML for season 3. (Full details in TMLregistration-season3.doc below)

Registration period.

  • June 20 - July 25 - Teams send all fully completed registration documents and items by e-mail/post, and complete the team registration fee payment.
    * Please note that if you have, or might have players that you want to register after July 25, that's fine, but for the time being please register your core members - more than 12 players - confirmed for next season, rather than delaying registration due to ifs, buts and maybes.
  • August - TML issue teams with TML member's cards and invoice for player registration.
  • September - Teams receive TML cards and official agreement signed by TML... and the league can start
