Mitsuike-Koen Soccer Ground
  • Surface: dirt ground 9:00am - 5:00pm.
  • Facilities: changing room with cold showers(no lockers),toilets & vending machines.
  • Parking: Paid parking available.
  • Location: 15 min. by taxi or bus from Tsurumi Station on the JR KeihinTohoku line.
    Buses from the stations:
    From JR Tsurumi station west exit. Bus for Kajiyama 15 min ride, get off at Mitsuike Koen Kita-mon, 3 min walk to the park
    From JR Shin-Yokohama, Bus terminal 7 for Tsurumi Station 15 min ride, get off at Mitsuike Koen Kita-mon, 3 min walk to the park
    MAP
    Tel: 045-581-0287
  • Rules: Teams may have to move goals before and after the match. Please follow instruction from the personnel at the park.
    No smoking! When teams & supporters leave after the match, they will make sure to clean up around the ground, removing all rubbish. Please follow all regulations at grounds (especially smoking!)
