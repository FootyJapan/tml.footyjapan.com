Oi Futo Dai Ni Soccer Ground

  • Surface: Large artificial floodlit pitch, can be used 9:00am - 9:00pm. New artificial turf pitch with long blade fibers & good cushioning surface.
  • Facilities: Changing rooms with hot showers, toilets & ¥100 lockers. Vending machines and convenience store outside of ground for drinks & snacks.
  • Parking: Carpark next to baseball stadium or another across the park from the ground.
  • Location: 15 minutes walk from Oikeibajomae Station on the Tokyo monorail.(2 stops from Hamamatsucho Stn) MAP
  • Rules: When teams & supporters leave after the match, they will make sure to clean up around the ground and changing rooms, removing all rubbish. Please follow all regulations at grounds (especially smoking!).
