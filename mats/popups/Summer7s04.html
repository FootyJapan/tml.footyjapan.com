TML & Footy Japan Summer Sevens

The Summer 7's

  • When: Saturday July 31st 2004
  • Time: Following the FJ Cup Final, 3:00 - 8:30pm
  • Where: Oi Futo Dai Ni football stadium.
  • Teams: Teams from both within and outside of Tokyo Metropolis League are Welcome. Players will be able to register & play in ONLY ONE team in this tournament.
  • Format: 4 Groups of 3 teams, then Knockout. Top 2 go to Quarter-finals, Semis & Finals. Bottom team to plate.
  • Cost: Team registration will be ¥20,000. Minimum of 3 matches per team.
  • Registration Period: Registration will end July 24th, to register, please CONTACT US.
  • Special Rules: 2 x 7 a side pitches will be set up, across the normal pitch. regular FIFA rules will be played but no off-sides. Games will be 10 minutes each way.
