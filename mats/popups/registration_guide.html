Team Registration guide

The following is a guide to registering your team and your members with the TML for season 4.
The process is relatively quick and simple and if completed in a timely manner will greatly aid the smooth running of the TML admin department.
If you have any queries or questions then feel free to give us a call at: 03-3770-0288 or send us an e-mail to: paco@footyjapan.com, we'll be happy to help. By registering with TML, the team accepts the rules and regulations in their entirety posted on the TML website. TML strongly advises all teams to read the rules before committing to Season 4.

Registration period.

  •  July 1 – July 15 – Teams send all fully completed registration documents and items by e-mail, and pay team registration fees (* Please note teams are being asked to register a minimum of 12-players by the due date – this is mandatory for all teams. After July 15th teams may register additional players at will, but the initial registration period (July 1 – 15) will not be extended as this will cause the whole admin. operation to get backed up before the league starts.
  • July 31 - TML issue teams invoices for player registration (To please be paid by August 20th)
  • August – Teams receive TML cards and the league can start

Registration fees & payment.

  • 1st Division teams: ¥35,000 / 2nd Division teams ¥25,000
    Team registration fee is to please be paid in full by July 15 into the following bank A/C:
    Bank of Tokyo-Mitsubishi UFJ, Shibuya branch (135), Regular A/C 3648175 , FOOTY JAPAN Ltd.
    Should you need an invoice issued for the registration fee, please inform us when registering your team.
  • Players (supporters, friends, family, colleagues, etc., who wish to be TML members): ¥2,000 - for which they will receive a TML member card entitling them to discounts at a variety of TML sponsors’ establishments.
    * Please encourage your players to show/use their TML cards as much as possible to help TML to further enhance the community infrastructure.
    Player registration fees will be included in the July invoice. (To be issued on July 31 and to please be paid in full by August 20).

Registration documents required.
(Please download the 2-requisite forms from here)

  • Complete registration form (Registration.xls) in its entirety, on behalf of your players and anyone else wanting to be a TML member (on the second .xls sheet provided therein) and e-mail form to paco@footyjapan.com before July 15
  • Complete TML Website form (Web_team_info.doc) as necessary and kindly submit your latest team photo and team information, unless there are no changes to be made to the photo and information already posted on the site (if no changes are to be made please inform us) and e-mail to paco@footyjapan.com before July 15
  • TML administration dept. requires photos of all players (and others requiring TML members’ cards) registering for Season 4, these, if we don’t already have them, must be e-mailed to paco@footyjapan.com in jpeg format, before July 15

During the Season

  • Team Sheet: The form TeamSheet.xls is to be submitted to paco@footyjapan.com no later than the following WEDNESDAY after match days with players first and last names typed in the order that they were registered (copying and pasting the players names from your Registration.xls file makes this process easy), this helps TML administration dept. keep track of the TML match statistics. Please also ensure that referees have full names (first and last) of goal scorers and carded players at the end of each match.
    The template for the team sheet is available on the TML website.·               
  • New Players: When a new player (non-TML registered player) plays his/her 3rd TML match, please make sure to submit the player registration information to paco@footyjapan.com TML will provide a TML card and will charge ¥2,000 yen for that player.
  • Payment: Match fees -¥15,000 yen/match for all league matches (¥12,000 yen/friendly match)
    Footy Japan Ltd. will issue invoices for the matches played at the end of every month. Teams are to pay the fees in full by due date as per their invoice.

If there are any questions or queries about any of the above, please e-mail paco@footyjapan.com.

Thank you in advance for your time, effort and cooperation on these important matters.

TML Administration
