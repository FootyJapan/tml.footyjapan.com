Futako Tamagawa (Shibuya-ku & Setagaya-ku) Soccer grounds

Shibuya-ku (Kumin) Soccer ground

Setagaya-ku (Ryokuchi) Soccer grounds
  • Surface: 3 Grass/dirt grounds 2 owned by Setagaya-ku, the other by Shibuya-ku
  • Facilities: Toilets, NO changing room, showers or lockers.
  • Parking: Carpark close to grounds.
  • Location: 10 minutes walk from Futako Tamagawa station on the Den en toshi Line(15 mins from Shibuya) & Oi Machi Line
  • Rules: When teams & supporters leave after the match, they will make sure to clean up around the ground and changing rooms, removing all rubbish. Please follow all regulations at grounds (especially smoking!).
