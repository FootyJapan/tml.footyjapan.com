Hodogaya Koen Soccer ground - grass
  • Surface: New long blade artificial turf Soccer/Rugby pitch AND Grass Football pitch, both can be used ALL WEATHER.
  • Facilities: Changing room, Showers.
  • Parking: Parking available.
  • Park Office: 045-331-5321.
  • Location: About 10 mins by bus from Hodogaya station on the JR Yokosuka Line or from Hoshikawa on the Sotetsu line.
    Ground Website or MAP
  • Rules: Teams must prepare the ground before games start (move goals, draw lines etc.). When teams & supporters leave after the match, they will make sure to clean up around the ground, removing all rubbish.
