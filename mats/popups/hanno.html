Hanno Misugidai Soccer Ground
  • Surface: New artificial Turf.
  • Facilities: small changing room, NO showers.
  • Parking: Free parking available, 20~30 cars.
  • Location: About 1.2 km from (Seibu) Hanno station's SOUTH exit.
    It is about 5~10 mins by Bus/Taxi
    Serviced by the Seibu-Ikebukuro line
    From Ikebukuro 50 min by Regular  express(kyuko)/35-40 min by Limited express(Tokyu).
    Ground Website or MAP
  • Rules: When teams & supporters leave after the match, they will make sure to clean up around the ground, removing all rubbish. Please follow all regulations at grounds (especially smoking!)

    *Please make sure to remove corner flags after the match and return to the main store cupboard located next to the pitch behind covered seating area.
    Teams should also be aware that there is little in the way of changing facilities so please take the necessary precautions in terms of a warm change of clothes.
