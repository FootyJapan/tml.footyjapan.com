Tennozu Isle (Shinagawa-ku) Soccer Ground
  • Surface: Large artificial floodlit pitch, can be used 9:00am - 9:00pm. New artificial turf pitch with long blade fibers & good cushioning surface
  • Facilities: Changing rooms with hot showers, toilets & lockers. Vending machines inside of main building for drinks. Tel: 03-3471-4573, add: Higashi Shinagawa 5-8-4
    Website
  • Parking: Carpark next to complex.
  • Location: Take the monorail to Tennozu Isle station (about a 3 minute walk) or take the JR RInkai line to Tennozu Isle station (1 minute walk from exit B) MAP
  • Rules: When teams & supporters leave after the match, they will make sure to clean up around the ground and changing rooms, removing all rubbish. Please follow all regulations at grounds (especially smoking!).
