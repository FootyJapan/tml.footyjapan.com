Tokyo University Gotenshita Soccer Ground
  • Surface: Large new generation artificial all weather/ pitch.
  • Facilities: Changing rooms with hot showers, toilets & lockers. Vending machines inside of main building for drinks.
  • Parking: Paid parking next to hospital.
  • Getting There:
    BY TRAIN: Take the Marunouchi line to Hongo San Chome Station and the ground is about 7 mins walk in the Gotenshita Memorial Arena, right in front of the Hospital.

    BY TAXI: Tell the driver to go to Tokyo University Hospital and the Gotenshita ground is next to it.

  • Rules: When teams & supporters leave after the match, they will make sure to clean up around the ground and changing rooms, removing all rubbish. Please follow all regulations at grounds (especially smoking!).
