Shane FC represents a community of like-minded people who enjoy both Footy and the Razz. At the end of any given match you will invariably find us Shane lads drinking a well earned beer and welcoming anyone in sight to come and join the party.

We have recently returned from our End of Season tour to Taiwan (06/09) were a squad of 15 Shane teachers competed against The Mighty Shane FC in the inaugural Shane Cup match in Taipei. We also played a friendly against Taipei’s leading foreign team Taipei City FC with the aim of building bridges between respective Shane teams and also the TML and Taipei’s TPL and BML.

The ethos of Shane FC is not only as an outlet for Shane teachers to play the beautiful game but also for non Shane teachers to play and compete. Our player of the year was Masa “Masimo” Sato, who was magnificent all season as were our entire squad in achieving a 3rd place finish in our Division. Our Top goal-scorer with 15 league goals was Giles “The Bull” Mitchell and our most improved award went to David “D-Razz” Bennett. We also competed in the New Year and March 7’s(Finalists in the Plate) and the Footyjapan Magnificent 7’s Summer league. For those interested in joining the Shane machine please contact our Manager Dan Kelly at shanefc@hotmail.com.
