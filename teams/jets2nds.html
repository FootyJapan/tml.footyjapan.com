The Saitama JETs 2nd Team was founded in 2007 by John Rayner and Deryk Bliss to ensure football for the increasingly large number of players in the Saitama Jets squad. The team's principles are ones of fair play, great team spirit and occasionally slick football. The squad is a family oriented one, but the desire is to remain competitive until we are over 40 and we try to achieve this by augmenting youngsters with the fat & baldies. The team also participates in the summer and autumn Nagano tournaments, as well as regular futsal competitions.

Uniform: Home: Blue Shirts, Blue Shorts & Blue Socks. Away: White Shirts, Blue Shorts & Blue Socks.
