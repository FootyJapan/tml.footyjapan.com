The King George football team was founded way back in 2008 by the now deceased Hugo Stronbowlio as a final throw of the fitness dice and to halt the pickling process that had begun in earnest on his eighteenth birthday.
Sadly, Hugo never lived long enough to see his team play, as he died of a massive coronary minutes before the kick-off of the very first match. As a result, prostitutes have now been banned from the King George dressing room until after the final whistle. In tribute to their dead leader, the King George football team ,have adopted a style of play that can best be described as “post mortem”, The formation is based on the popular music video “thriller” by pop prince Michael Jackson. Opposition teams have found it most accommodating.
Due to the high mortality rate in the King George football team, we are always looking for fresh blood and would be interested in speaking to anyone who has played international standard football to join us (not you Robbie Savage). Please give generously.
