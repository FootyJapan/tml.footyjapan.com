Robert Walters Clash Founded: 2004
The club (Geckoes) was formed during 2002 with the first game being a 2-1 victory against Temple University on October 13. We entered the IFFL where we played for two seasons before joining the TML. The Geckoes started the TML in Division 2 and managed to gain promotion following a 3-0 win against Shane on the last game of the season. Since gaining promotion, the Geckoes have gone from strength to strength, establishing themselves as one of the leading Division 1 teams and winning the FJ Cup in 2007.
After the Geckoes were promoted the Clash were formed to take their place in Division 2. A strong mix of footballing ability and social skills has led the Clash to becoming one of the most popular teams in the league. Relegated on the last game of the season in 2008/2009 it's hoped they will be able to win Division 3 and get their first trophy.
