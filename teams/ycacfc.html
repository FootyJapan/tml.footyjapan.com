YC&AC 1st Team
Originally founded in 1868, YCAC is generally reckoned to have played the 1st football match in Japan vs Kobe in 1888, a fixture stilled played annually. We have played at our current site in Yamate since 1912 and have survived the 1923 Kanto Earthquake, being bombed by the USAF and the 1st 6 seasons of the TML. We are very proud reigning Division 1 Champions

We are grateful to our sponsors Mogambos and Magellan Financial Planning.
