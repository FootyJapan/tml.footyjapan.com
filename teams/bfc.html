The British Football Club, Tokyo, was founded in 1980 and is one of the oldest foreign football club in Japan. In our 26-year history we have won all the major football tournaments in the Tokyo area (some of them several times), and have toured to Shizuoka, Naeba, Ueda, Philippines and Shanghai.
The BFC encompasses two teams, the BFC and BFC Vagabonds, with a total squad membership of over 50 players. However with regular turnover of players due to people leaving the country, we are always looking for useful new players. If you are interested in joining, please contact us
