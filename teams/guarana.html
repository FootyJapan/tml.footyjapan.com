Team Guarana Republic is from Sapporo, Hokkaido. The team was born in 1999 as one of the Teini Healthful Club’s soccer teams. Teini Health Club sponsors a football feastival, the S-Cup, every August in Sapporo. Gurana Republic were the 2000 S-Cup Champions. The team has been playing in Tokyo since 2005.
