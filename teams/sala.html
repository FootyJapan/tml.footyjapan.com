SALA FC

Back Row: Peter Swinney, Ian Jelley, Shiva Tawari, Shun Ono, Paul Clarke, Stu Anderson, Scott Thomson, Guido Geisler(c).
Front Row: Shigeru Chino, Take Kurome, Lenny Tui, Steve Brooke-Smith, Daniel Bard, Toby Long, Ryotaro Suzuki, Sam Matthews.

Sala FC is a Chiba based team that was established in 1997.
Since joining the IFFL in 2002 the team has gained promotion from Division 2 as champions, finished 3rd in its first season in Division 1 and were beaten finalists in the World Motors Cup in 2003. Sala FC has also participated in other competitions and won the Highland games 5 a-side and the UNICEF 7 a-side tournaments.
