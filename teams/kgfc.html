Kwansei Gakuin Football Club (KGFC) was reunited on July 2004. Most of the players are the members of Kwansei Gakuin Univ. Football Club who won the championship of Kansai Gakusei League for the first time in 25 years. We also invited talented players from diverse interchanges. This is a new team but is a team who carry on the tradition as one of the oldest football club in Japan.
