YC&AC 2nd Team
Originally founded in 1868, YCAC is generally reckoned to have played the 1st football match in Japan vs Kobe in 1888, a fixture stilled played annually. We have played at our current site in Yamate since 1912 and have survived the 1923 Kanto Earthquake, being bombed by the USAF and the 1st 3 seasons of the TML.
We are grateful to our sponsors Instinet and Magellan
