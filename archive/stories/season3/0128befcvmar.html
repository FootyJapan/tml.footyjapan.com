BEFC Hold Maritizio to Draw

OiFuto dirt, Sat 28th January. British Embassy’s improving run of form continued as they frustrated Mauritzio at Oi Futo to record their third draw of the season.
Mauritzio produced most of the attacking football in the first half, but failed to even deliver a serious shot on target. British Embassy for all their efforts weren’t much better.
Embassy’s Steve Lidbury and Tetsuo also failed to convert decent chances in the first half.
BEFC offered more after the break, and came close to scoring when Kotaro unleashed a dipping shot that cannoned off the cross bar. 
Mauritzio almost stole the points at the death, when their striker cut inside the penalty box from the right and curled a goal bound shot towards the far left corner, only to find the superb Keith Crowley finger tip it round the post.
Man of the match – Keith Crowley – Was only called upon once, but was there when needed.
