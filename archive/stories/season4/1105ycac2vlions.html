YCAC2 Fights Back Twice to Earn a Point.

YC&AC, Sun 5th Nov. As always the TML Division 2 is proving somethig of a challenge for YCAC2 this season.  It seems that the standard of teams is generally improving and there is no such thing as an easy 3 points.  Exactly how it should be!

We went into this game having had 2 straight defeats - one to AFC (who never used to beat anybody!) and one to a revitalised Vagabonds team (with a few BFC ringers in it).    We were also missing a few players in midfield due to work commitments, plus Lions were an unknown quantity who had done Shane 5-2 in their last outing.

A fairly even first half finally swung the Lions way with one of the now traditional defensive mistakes that we've been perfecting for some time in the Seconds.  To be fair I don't remember us troubling their keeper much that half, it was mostly played in our half due to the also-traditional breeze blowing straight down the pitch.  And the Lions had come close a few times.

Second half had barely kicked off when Viv (first game back and looking very comfortable) slotted a great ball through to Tom on the left.  He rounded the fullback and then knocked over a cracking cross which caused confusion in the Lions defence.  I couldn't see who scored but it wasn't one of our players.  1-1 and game on.   We probably played our best football from this point onwards - Alex in particular was running his socks off in midfield.  I remember thinking we could do this.....and then almost immediately another bit of messy play let to Lions taking the lead once more.  Still cometh the hour, cometh the man.  Per took responsibility for a free kick with 5 minutes left - must have been 30 meters out - and absolutely buried in in the top left hand corner.  A smashing goal at any level.

A good match which was very ably refereed.
 
Best wishes to Rob O`Grady for a speedy recovery.  (Rob is currently being investigated by the Union of Centre Halves for being stretchered off the field and taking an ambulance to hospital.)

Steve McKie
